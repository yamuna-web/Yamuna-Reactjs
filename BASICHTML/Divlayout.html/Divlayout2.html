Telangana is a state in the southern coastal region of India. It is the seventh-largest state and the tenth-most populous in the country.In 1971 people voted for Telangana by electing 11 of 13 seats to parliment.

Telugu, one of India's classical languages, is the primary official language and the most widely spoken language. Hydarabad is the capital of andhra pradesh.
